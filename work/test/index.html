The Treaty Map

Indigenous Perspectives on Treaties in Canada

Covering every single treaty from 1763 to present, the Yellowhead Treaty Map aims to challenge the commmonly held view of treaties as land surrenders and offers a comprehensive, interactive web-based learning and teaching tool, grounded in Indigenous perspectives of treaties.
