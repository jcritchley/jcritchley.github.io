Development Opportunity


As an experimental side project at Ratio.City, we developed an interactive visualization of City of Toronto Development Opportunity based on current planning policy framework. It was an experiment to help demonstrate what policy actually looks like on the ground.

We used 3 main indicators of Development Potential within the City of Toronto:

  • Must have Mixed Use, Apartment or Regeneration OP Land use designation,
  • Located within a Provincially designation Urban Growth Centre,
  • Located within 500m of an existing or under construction MTS

Combining these indicators in a “Development Opportunity Score” and mapping with 3D Building Massing it is clear there is remarkably little land available in the City for intensification - and a lot of that either already has high density use or a development proposal on it.

An interesting take away from the exercise was highlighting that opportunity areas that were identified already fairly built up and already have have high density developments. It was easy to convey this through the use of 3D building massing, all possible due to Mapbox GL extrusion styling.
